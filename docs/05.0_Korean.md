## Goal
Can Protei avoid immediate collision? Can the electronic be modular and really cheap? Can the line of the body be simplified?
Developed at the [NABI media art center](http://www.nabi.or.kr/main/main.nab) in Seoul, Korea with the [randomwalks](http://randomwalks.org/public_lab/randomwalkshome/) team.
